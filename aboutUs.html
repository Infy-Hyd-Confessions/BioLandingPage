About Us & Who we are

So basically, Infy Stories started as a random Instagram page (@infy_stories) where Infoscians could rant, vent, share stories, and ask stuff — all without anyone knowing who you are. And somehow... it actually blew up 😅

Think of it as your anonymous campus buddy. Wanna know about hikes? Ask away. Got a funny hostel story from Mysore? Drop it. Confused about some policy nobody understands? Same, but at least we can talk about it here.

We're on Instagram and Telegram, and yes, we do read everything 👀

Just one rule — don't be a jerk. Keep it clean, keep it fun, and don't write stuff that'll get HR on our case. Cool? Cool.
